Tell us about the task, process, or headache you want solved. Be as specific or as vague as you want — we'll figure out the rest.

What happens with your submission

We review every request personally. If it's something we can build — we'll reach back with good news and a clear picture of what it would take. If it's outside our scope, we'll still respond with specific suggestions for what to do and who can help. Either way, you hear from us.

More detail helps us give you a more specific answer. But even a sentence is enough to start.

We respond to every submission — whether we can help or not.

What kinds of things can you submit?

Anything that feels manual, repetitive, or like it should already happen automatically. Here are the kinds of things people submit:

  • We copy leads from our website into our CRM by hand every morning
  • After every client meeting I spend an hour writing up notes and action items
  • We have to manually pull a status report every Friday from three different tools
  • Scheduling subcontractors requires 20 phone calls every week
  • We review the same 4 documents manually before every project kicks off
