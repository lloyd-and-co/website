HOW WE WORK

We work three ways.
Pick the one that fits.

Some clients want a tool their team runs forever. Some want an agent that just does the work and reports back. Some don't know what they need yet — they just know something's broken. All three are on the table.

📊
A Tool For Your Team

Something your team opens and uses — every day, on their own.

An app, a dashboard, an Excel model, a client portal — whatever shape fits the problem. We build it from scratch, wire it into the tools you already use, and hand it off. It's yours. Your team drives it.

"Like the Client Selections Portal we built for a design-build firm — a 2,944-product wizard their team now runs in every meeting."

Best for: Teams that want control in their own hands.

🕐
An Agent That Works For You

Not a tool you use — something that does the task and hands you the result.

Think of it like hiring for one job, minus the hiring. The agent runs on its own schedule — daily, weekly, whenever the trigger fires — and you get the output: a report, a follow-up sent, a schedule updated, a lead worked. You don't log in and do anything. It just happens.

"Like the subcontractor dispatch agent that handles scheduling overnight, or the Monday-morning ops digest that's in your inbox before you're at your desk."

Best for: Teams that want the work gone, not new software to learn.

💬
Tell Us What's Broken

Don't know if it's a tool, an agent, or something else? That's our job to figure out — not yours.

Describe the actual pain. We'll tell you straight whether AI can fix it, and if so, what that looks like. If it's not a fit, we'll say that too, and point you somewhere that can help. No guessing on your end, no overselling on ours.

Best for: Anyone who isn't sure yet.
