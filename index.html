Buildertrend Automation Suite

AI Document Audit Tool

Subcontractor Dispatch & Scheduling Agent
A 24/7 agent that handles sub availability, assignments, and conflicts overnight.

Deployed as a persistent background agent. When a job opens or a schedule changes, the agent checks availability, sends assignments, logs confirmations, and updates the master schedule — without a coordinator making calls. Runs overnight so the morning starts with everything sorted.

Client Onboarding & Selections Portal

Weekly Operations Digest

Automated Proposal & Quote Generator
Turn a 5-minute intake form into a polished, branded proposal — automatically.

A client fills out a short intake form — project type, scope, budget range, timeline. The system pulls from your service library and pricing logic, assembles a fully formatted proposal in your brand, and sends it to the client's inbox within minutes. No one writes a proposal from scratch again.

Proposal turnaround: hours → minutes · Consistent pricing, every time
8
Review Request & Reputation Automation
Every completed job automatically asks for a review — at exactly the right moment.

When a job is marked complete, the system waits a configurable number of days, then sends a personalized review request via email or SMS. It tracks who responded, who didn't, and sends a single follow-up to non-responders. Integrates with Google, Yelp, or any review platform. The best time to ask for a review is right after a great experience — this makes sure it happens every single time.

3–5x more reviews without anyone remembering to ask
9
Invoice Follow-Up & Collections Sequence
Overdue invoices chase themselves — professionally, automatically, without awkwardness.

When an invoice passes its due date, the system automatically sends a polite reminder on day 1, a firmer follow-up on day 7, and an escalation on day 14 — all in your voice, with the invoice attached. Payments received instantly stop the sequence. No one has to remember to follow up, and no client relationship gets awkward because a human had to send a collection email.

Average collections time cut by 40% · Zero awkward manual follow-ups
10
New Employee Onboarding System
From offer accepted to fully onboarded — without HR manually managing every step.

When a new hire is added to your system, an automated sequence kicks off: welcome email with first-day details, document requests (W-4, I-9, direct deposit), equipment provisioning requests to IT, a manager checklist, and a 30-day check-in reminder. Each step only triggers when the previous one is complete. Nothing falls through. Nobody has to remember what step they're on.

Onboarding time cut in half · Consistent experience for every new hire
11
Meeting Notes → Action Items Pipeline
Every meeting ends with a summary, decisions logged, and tasks assigned — automatically.

After a call or meeting, the transcript or recording is passed to an AI agent that extracts key decisions, open questions, and action items — assigns them to the right people, adds them to your project management tool, and emails a clean summary to all attendees. No more "who was supposed to handle that?" conversations three days later.

Zero dropped action items · Full meeting record searchable in seconds
12
Contract Generation from Templates
Contracts write themselves from the details already in your system.

When a deal is won or a project is greenlit, the system pulls the relevant details — client name, project scope, pricing, dates — from wherever they live (CRM, spreadsheet, intake form) and populates a fully formatted contract from your template. Ready to send for signature in under a minute. No copy-pasting, no wrong version, no missing fields.

Contract generation: 45 min → under 2 min · Zero data entry errors
